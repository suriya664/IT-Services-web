About CloudGuard Technologies

Empowering businesses with secure, scalable, and innovative IT solutions since 2013

Our Story

Building trust through excellence in cybersecurity and IT services

Founded in 2013 in Bengaluru, CloudGuard Technologies has grown from a small cybersecurity consultancy to a leading IT services provider serving over 500 organizations across India and Southeast Asia.

Our mission is to make enterprise-grade security and cloud infrastructure accessible to businesses of all sizes. We believe that every organization deserves robust protection against cyber threats and the agility that cloud technology provides.

With a team of 150+ certified professionals and partnerships with major cloud providers, we deliver comprehensive solutions that combine security, efficiency, and innovation.
